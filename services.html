My Offered Services

What I Offer

Software Requirement Elicitation

The software Requirement eliciattion is the most important ascpect of a project. To do this process successfully, I conduct stakeholder meetings, requirement worshops , document analysis along with brainstorming sessions.

Requirements Analysis

To do the Requirement analysis, I choose the best modeling technique to support the company's software development approach in order to make sure that the requirements, alternative solututions and requirement gaps are well identified.

Requirement Documentation

I create requirement documents including functional requirement specifications, stakeholder analysis, business analysis plan, current state analysis and change management documents. I also write user manuals to benefit the end users.

Risk and Issue Management

I publish project status reports and revise risk plans that can be happen in a major change of project schedule. I also maintaine Change Request logs, Incident logs and Project Governanace plans to make sure that all the issues are identified before the project is deployed.

Requirements Communication

I make process maps and conduct presentations with Developers and QA Enginners to make sure that the requirements are well identified. I also conduct presentations to stakeholders to inform the current state of requirements.

Quality Assurance

I create detailed, comprehensive and well structured test plans and test cases. I also do test case execution along with Database Verification, Defect Verification and User Acceptable Testing.
